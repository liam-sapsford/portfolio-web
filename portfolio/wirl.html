WIRL WORLD

a collaboration project between me and a friend from university. He provided the character designs, and I built the models then created these 3 styles in blender

These are greatly inspired by designer art toys and I aimed to make them feel like they could be real figurines

WIRL WORLD is created by Gus Wilson, a fellow media design graduate. you can find his work on instagram: @gusssy_wilson
