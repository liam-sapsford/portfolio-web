gambit

a cute, low poly character I designed in blender for a group game project called Runner's Gambit. This is the titular Runner, named Gambit.

below is a demonstration of the game. I designed the entire game's concept and premise, and developed all the graphics except the skies and animations. I designed many of the the levels, and also programmed key mechanics such as character movement, edit mode, and the progress bar.

models made in blender, game built in unreal engine 4 with blueprints
